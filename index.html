About

An interdisciplinary effort against pancreatic cancer

The SCIE-PANC project unites researchers from six countries across three continents to improve treatment outcomes in pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma (PDAC) — one of the most aggressive and therapy-resistant cancers known.

Our consortium brings together experts in 3D-epigenomics, integrative bioinformatics, mechanobiology, computational modeling, drug screening, and PDAC biology, all working in concert toward new therapeutic strategies.
